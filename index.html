Wedding Festivities

The wedding celebrations will commence in Surat, where love and joy will fill the air as families and friends come together to honor Mokshi and Rushi’s union. This joyous occasion will be steeped in rich traditions and unforgettable moments. Here’s a glimpse into the festivities:

  • Garba:

    The Garba is a vibrant musical celebration that unites family and friends through dance and song. Expect a night filled with lively performances, laughter, and memories that will be cherished forever. This is the time for everyone to let loose, have fun, and celebrate the love shared by Mokshi and Rushi.

  • Mehndi:

    The Mehndi ceremony is a colorful celebration where henna designs are intricately applied to the bride and her loved ones. This ritual symbolizes joy and beauty, creating an atmosphere of creativity and festivity as everyone comes together to share in the excitement and anticipation of the wedding.

  • Wedding Ceremony:

    The wedding ceremony is the heart of the celebrations, where Mokshi and Rushi will pledge their love and commitment to each other in front of family and friends. This sacred event will incorporate beautiful rituals and traditions that reflect their love, culture, and family values, making it a memorable occasion for everyone involved.
